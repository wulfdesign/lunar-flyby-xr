# 📜 Project History: A Journey Through Space and Time

**[⚠️ WORK IN PROGRESS]**

This project is the culmination of a lifelong vision for space exploration and the accessibility of its wonders through immersive technology.

## 🏔️ The Alaska Roots
The journey began with the **Alaska Academy of Science**, planning for space-based exploration and humanity's future in the cosmos. 
*   **Astrophysics at UAF:** Early foundational work taking astrophysics at the **University of Alaska Fairbanks (UAF)** and its **Geophysical Institute (GI)**, a global leader in aurora research.
*   **Space Base Research:** Developing ideas for how humanity can sustain its future beyond the stars.

## 🌑 High Desert Eclipse (2017)
Fast forward to the **2017 Total Solar Eclipse**, Larry James filmed a 360-degree timelapse in the **Oregon high desert**. 
*   **Result:** The immersive film **"High Desert Eclipse"** was released on the **Meta Quest App Lab** and **YouTube**, providing a "once-in-a-lifetime" experience to thousands of people who couldn't be in the path of totality.
*   **Impact:** A powerful tool for STEM education, schools, and individuals to witness the majestic solar corona in 4K.

## 🎈 UpLiftVR 'Maiden Flight' (2018)
Larry's studio, **UpLiftVR**, developed the **'Maiden Flight' Balloon Ride**, an experimental VR experience that headlined the **Seattle International Film Festival (SIFF) VR Zone** in 2018.

## 🌙 The dearMoon Proposal
When the **dearMoon project** was first announced, Larry submitted a proposal to join the mission as a world-class creative. Though the project was ultimately cancelled in 2024, the vision for a lunar flyby remained.

## 🚀 Artemis: The Free Return (2026)
With the launch of **Artemis II** on April 1, 2026, the technology finally reached a point—through Three.js, WebXR, and agentic AI—where this vision could be realized as a public-facing, interactive prototype. 

*This timeline is a living document, documenting the intersection of personal passion and the next great age of human spaceflight.*
